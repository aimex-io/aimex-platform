PAP-117Planning · Onboarding wizard options

Reframe onboarding around
building agent teams for work.

Today the wizard reads like incorporating a company — Mission → Team lead → “Launch company” → “{company} is live!” Starting a company is one use case, but the product is really about spinning up and directing a team of agents to get work done. Below are four ways to reframe the wizard. Each is shown as a sequence of screens, built on the live brand system — tokens, type, and the capsule motif (“the capsule is the agent”). Option 4 realizes the board’s own wireframe. No product code changed — this is a proposal for the board to pick a direction.

4 options Animated screen sequences Brand tokens + capsule motif Hi-fi, non-interactive Awaiting board direction

The reframe

From “start a company” to “staff the work.”

Same flow length, different center of gravity. The shifts that carry across all four options:

Team, not company

“Launch company” · “{company} is live!” → “Build your team” · “Your team is on it”. A company is one shape a team can take, not the headline.

Work is the why

Abstract “mission” → a concrete job to get done. You’re hiring agents to do work, so lead with the work.

The capsule is the agent

Every agent is a capsule that fills with color and comes online. A consistent, ownable visual for “an agent exists now” — replacing the 🚀 rocket.

You stay in control

“CEO” by default → a team lead you direct. The lead drives the plan; you approve who joins. Carries [PAP-63] forward.

For reference

What the wizard does today

ui/src/components/OnboardingWizard.tsx — a 3-tab stepper, company-centric copy, default agent name “CEO”, exits on a rocket celebration:

1 · Mission — “Define your mission” → 2 · Team lead — “Hire your team lead”, default name CEO → 3 · Launch — 🚀 “{company} is live!” · “Launch company” → chat

All four options keep the same backend handoff (a lead agent + a planning chat). They change copy, iconography (capsule vs rocket), and step framing — not the underlying create-agent-then-plan flow.

For the board

Open questions

  • Which direction? 1 Outcome-first · 2 Build your team · 3 Concierge chat · 4 The capsule wizard — or a hybrid (e.g. Option 4’s capsule lifecycle + Option 1’s “what do you want to get done?” opener)?
  • How far does the capsule motif go? Adopt the dotted→solid→filled capsule as the canonical “agent status” visual across the app (not just onboarding)?
  • Keep “company” anywhere? Starting a company is still a valid use case — do we keep it as a selectable team type, or drop the word entirely from setup?
  • Default lead name. “Chief of staff” vs “Team lead” vs keep “CEO” as the default. Which reads best for a team-of-agents framing?
  • Scope of build. Confirm this round is copy + capsule motif + step framing on the existing flow — no new backend. Then I can decompose the chosen direction into implementation subtasks.
